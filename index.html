On Safari brings the raw beauty of African wildlife into your home through stunning photographic fine art prints. Each image is captured in the wild — lions, elephants, zebras, hippos — and printed to museum-quality standards.

Whether you're looking for a statement piece for your living room or a meaningful gift, On Safari's collections span the breadth of Africa's most iconic animals and landscapes.

Ubuntu Wellness

Health Spa · Float Therapy · Homeopathy — Cape Town

Wellness & Beauty

A Sanctuary for Mind, Body & Spirit

Ubuntu Wellness is Cape Town's most holistic health sanctuary. From float therapy and spa treatments to homeopathy, vegan nutrition and stress management — this is where healing happens.

Rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are" — the centre offers a complete approach to wellness that treats the whole person.
