Ubuntu Wellness & Medi-Spa

Health Spa · Float Therapy · Homeopathy — Kloof Street, Cape Town

Wellness & Beauty

A Sanctuary for Mind, Body & Spirit

Ubuntu Wellness is Cape Town's most holistic health sanctuary. From float therapy and spa treatments to homeopathy, vegan nutrition and stress management — this is where healing happens.

Rooted in the African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are" — the centre offers a complete approach to wellness that treats the whole person.

🌳 Nokuphila — The Ubuntu Tree

In the heart of the Cape Town Medi-Spa courtyard stands Nokuphila — meaning she who gives life. This ancient tree is more than a landmark; she is the living soul of Ubuntu Wellness. Guests are invited to sit beneath her canopy, breathe deeply, and reconnect with nature before or after their treatments. She embodies the Ubuntu philosophy — that we are healed not alone, but together.
